Chakaria Upazila

Chakaria (Bengali: চকরিয়া) is an upazila of Cox's Bazar District in the Division of Chittagong, Bangladesh.

Chakaria thana was constituted in 1793 and was turned into an upazila in 1983. Chakaria Upazila is divided into Chakaria Municipality and 18 union parishads: Badarkhali, Baraitali, Bheola Manikchar, Bamo Bilchari, Chiringa, Demusia, Dulhazara, Fashiakhali, Harbang, Kaiarbil, Kakhara, Khuntakhali, Konakhali, Lakhyarchar, Paschim Bara Bheola, Purba Bara Bheola, Saharbil, and Surajpur Manikpur. The union parishads are subdivided into 48 mauzas and 212 villages. Chakaria Bazar Municipality is subdivided into 9 wards and 23 mahallas[wikipedia].
